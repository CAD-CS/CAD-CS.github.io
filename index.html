About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm a third year CS major with a passion for all things problem solving. I like all things software, but functional programming and languages (Haskell + F#) hold a special place in my heart. With regards to more "traditional" languages, I am most familiar Java and TypeScript (from projects) and C/C++ (from classes and labs). In the future, I hope to add C# and Rust to this ever growing list as well.

I also enjoy dabbling in a fair amount of game development. Other than being plain fun (at least when not dealing with bugs), I find that it helps considerably with my problem solving skills much more so than LeetCode as I get to solve real problems and gain hands-on experience. I can utilize a wide variety of skills that I have acquired over the years ranging from design patterns to UI design.

I also have non-tech related interests, such as language learning (日本語を話します) and corporate governance. I am particularly interested in the different types of organizational structures of corporations and the decision making processes that occur within.
