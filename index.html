About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm a third year CS major with a passion for all things problem solving. I like all things software, but functional programming and languages (Haskell + F#) hold a special place in my heart. With regards to more "traditional" languages, I am most familiar Java and TypeScript (from projects) and C/C++ (from classes and labs). In the future, I hope to add C# and Rust to this ever growing list as well.

I also have non-tech related interests, such as language learning (日本語を話します) and linguistics. I am quite keenly interested in East Asian languages and I aim to add Mandarin and Korean to my repertoire in the future.

Furthermore, I am currently working on the Ancient Voices project which aims to reconstruct dead languages using their more widely known cousins. For example, using Sanskrit and Latin to reconstruct Old Gothic words and vocabulary.
